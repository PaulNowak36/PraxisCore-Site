Introduction to PraxisCore

PraxisCore designs, governs and scales high-complexity healthcare systems, starting from clinical reality and operational constraints, and translating them into robust, investable and long-term platforms.

We partner with investors, healthcare groups and public institutions to structure, launch and operate advanced medical platforms, with a strong focus on nuclear medicine, cardiovascular diagnostics and highly regulated clinical environments.

What we do

PraxisCore intervenes across the entire lifecycle of healthcare assets — from strategic conception to long‑term governance.

Core activities

  • Healthcare platform design & commissioning
  • Medical governance & operational engineering
  • Board‑level medical and strategic advisory
  • Public–private healthcare structuring (PPP / hybrid models)
  • Selective co‑investment alongside partners

We do not provide generic consulting.
We build systems grounded in real‑world clinical operations.
